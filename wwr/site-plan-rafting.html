Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help visitors have a safe and fun experience of a lifetime. To do this, we have informational guides of the areas and location. There are different routes for all different types of parties so that anyone can join the fun! Look into our rentals if you think you forgot something. We have on-site stores with plenty of snacks, drinks, and more. We have campsites if you wish to enjoy the view off shore. Ask about our seasonal rates.

Audience

Lots of fun for everyone. We have wet and dry options. This includes rafting, hikes, campsites, and more. We host families, youth groups, company events, etc. Book here online with us or contact us for more information.

  1. Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on).
    • Our customers include families, youth groups, companies, etc. Pretty much anyone who is looking for adventure in the great outdoors under professional supervision by our company.
  2. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
    • This website provides customers with information about our rafting areas. Customers are also able to book reservations and find our contact information for questions and concerns.
  3. What are their needs that aren't met?
    • Please wear water clothes and shoes. We have a snack bar, but guests are welcome to bring outside food and drinks.
